My Heritage: Idlib

Idlib is more than just a city; it is a profound legacy of deep roots, an enduring spirit, and a remarkably rich history. Growing up with a strong connection to this authentic background has been the cornerstone of my identity, deeply influencing the person I am today. It is a place where ancient traditions blend with unwavering resilience, teaching me the true meaning of solidarity, strength, and hope. Every olive grove, every mountain breeze, and every story passed down through generations carries a piece of my heritage, shaping my values and inspiring my journey forward.

The Olive Groves

Idlib is famous for its ancient, beautiful olive trees that represent peace, blessing, and our deep roots in the land.

Darkoush Mountains

The breathtaking natural landscapes, green mountains, and the pure river of Darkoush showcase the natural beauty of my heritage.

Gaza Square

A symbol of solidarity. The people of Idlib always stand firmly with the truth and support just causes around the world.

Traditional Shuaibiyat

A famous, mouth-watering traditional dessert that represents our rich and unique culinary culture.
